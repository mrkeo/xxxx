<h4>About project</h4>

<img class="img-small mr-3 mt-2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/trolit/projectZero/storage/photos/front.jpg"/>

Extensive game made from scratch as a part of engineering work under <em>A „learn to program” aid as a game developed with Unity 3D</em> subject. Besides making enjoyable game, main project target was to develop GBL (game based learning) app that will interest it's players into meeting programming world and encourage them to discover raised subjects when surfing the net to find more information. Educational values of the game are mentioned <a href="https://github.com/trolit/projectZero/blob/master/README_en.md#educational-values" target="_blank">here</a>. Graphics: <a href="https://github.com/trolit/projectZero/blob/master/README_en.md#screens-from-the-game" target="_blank">[1]</a> and <a href="https://github.com/trolit/projectZero/blob/master/SHOWCASE_en.md#animated-screens" target="_blank">[2]</a>.
